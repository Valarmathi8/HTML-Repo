My Success

MySuccess is more than just a way to schedule appointments with your Academic Advisor.*
*To learn how to schedule an appointment with Academic Advising, Career Services, Bruin Scholars Success Coaches, and other groups on campus, check out our student tutorial videos on the Student Resources Page.
This online tool connects students to offices across campus that can support them in achieving their academic and personal goals, helps them better communicate with staff about their progress, and ensures that they always have someone ready to help if they want more support.
MySuccess prompts faculty to give regular feedback to students along with the tools to improve and an easy way for the student and advisor to close the loop with faculty once the feedback has been implemented.
